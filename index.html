Watermelon Juice

No text
  1. Get a cup of watermelon.

Simple Fries

No text
  1. Boil 2 baking patatos.
  2. Let it for 15-20 mins.
  3. Once boiled properly peel them off.
  4. Chop them into small cubes.
  5. Fry in a pan for five mins.
  6. Add salt and other seasonings.
  7. Your healthy fries are ready.

Tomato Garlic Sauce

No text
  1. Chop and boild 3 tomatos.
  2. Add half tea spoon salt.
  3. Chop 3 garlic cloves.
  4. Fry garlic cloves seperately.
  5. Mix those with tamatos.
  6. Cook the mixture for 15 mins.
